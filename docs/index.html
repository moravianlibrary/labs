MZK LABS

supports, inspires and promotes digital research activities
offers tools and data sets based on digital collections of the Moravian Library (MZK)
#innovation #creativity #experiments

The Moravian Library Labs is a library programme for innovations and experimentation in digital research practice and scholarship. In the Labs, you will find tools and data sets based on our digital collections, research case studies, digital resources, as well as selected research activities or crowdsourcing intentions of the library.

Dataset

Datasets based on our digitized collections, they consist of a small selection of bibliographic databases, digitised newspapers and maps.

Visit our GITHUB

Digital library

In the Digital Library you can find books, periodicals, manuscripts, maps, sound recordings, sheet music or graphics; access is restricted by copyright.

Visit our Digital Library

OCR Tools

PERO-OCR demonstration application allows users to automatically transcribe several types of printed and handwritten documents.

Georeferencing competition

The next round of the competition in georeferencing old maps was prepared on the website staremapy.cz. In less than two months, 67 volunteers actively participated in the competition, processing all 2,791 prepared maps.

Digitization

Digitization is one of the basic activities of the Moravian Library. Mass digitization has its origins in the project of the National Digital Library (NDK), where the Moravian Library was a partner of the National Library of the Czech Republic. Thanks to the National Digital Library project we scanned thousands of pages per day.
