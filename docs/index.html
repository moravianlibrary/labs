The Moravian Library Labs is a library programme for innovations and experimentation in digital research practice and scholarship. In the Labs, you will find tools and data sets based on our digital collections, research case studies, digital resources, as well as selected research activities or crowdsourcing intentions of the library.

News from labs.mzk.cz

International Conference on the History of Cartography. 

The Faculty of Sciences of the Masaryk University has recently joint the team of the bodies (Moravian Library, Czech Academy of Sciences, Charles University) that form the local organising committee of this very special event. It will take place in Prague and Brno in June 2026.

NAKI III programme

As part of NAKI III programme, the Moravian Library cooperates with other Czech research institutions on a number of projects, most of which aim at machine learning. The SemANT project seeks to develop tools able to identify topics in texts and thus enable full-text search at the level of textual meaning in digital documents. The OmniOMR project focuses on music document retrieval and automatic identification of musical notation in digital documents. Tools helping to recognise non-textual parts of digitised documents are developed by the Orbis Pictus project, which will make it possible to identify and analyse various graphic entities. Finally, Smart digiline project seeks to improve the efficiency of digitisation itself through machine learning.
